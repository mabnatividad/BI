  • Analysis
  • 1. Transparency increases the amount of donations by 33%. This means that our business that promotes transparency will really help.
  • 2. The average days people would be willing to help in an area is around 1 week.
  • 3. People are willing to pay 64% more when helping abroad than locally. This means that finding volunteers from abroad should not be that much a problem.
  • 4. As the willingness of those who helped below 20 times are relatively close, nothing much can be said. However, those that helped more than 20 times are less willing, which could mean that they were only forced to help most of the times.
  • 5. The willingness greatly affects the amount of donation. The more willing they are, the higher they are willing to donate.
  • 6. Those that have helped in the past with the three methods of In Kind, Volunteer Work, and Monetarily, are more willing to donate a higher amount.
  • 7. The number of people they are with does does not really have a major effect with their willingness. Although too little than 5 and more 30 shows that there is a lower willingness.
