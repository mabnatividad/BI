About us

Mission

To make aid-giving to Philippine disaster-areas a model for organizations and volunteers from around the world in terms of community relief and development impact, transparency, and documentation.

Vision

For people anywhere in the world to be able to give aid to disaster-stricken areas in the Philippines and know that what they gave was put to good use.


"Build-A-Better-Philippines through sustainable relief and community building efforts"
