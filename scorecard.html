Financial

OBJECTIVE MEASURES GREEN TARGET YELLOW TARGET RED TARGET INITIATIVES
TRANSPARENCY OF PROJECT FINANCIALS Financial reports must be available to public after project completion 95% of project financial reports must be posted after 1 month of project completion 85%-94% of project financial reports are posted after 1 month of project completion Below 85% of project financial reports are posted after 1 month of project completion -Create team to continuously update project reports and post on website
-Provide donors with a report on how their donations were spent
ACHIEVEMENT OF TARGET PROJECT DONATIONS Donation targets must be reached given a set period of time 90% and above of target project donations are collected within given time Collected donations of a project reaches only 80%-89% of target goal within given time Collected donations of a project reaches 75% or below of target goal within given time -Engage donors through countdown reminders and suggesting expiring projects to donate to
-Establish strong ties with CSR departments of large corporations
-Set a challenging but achievable goal and duration
SUPPORT OPERATION COSTS FROM PROJECT DONATIONS Percentage of operations costs covered by donations 70% of company operation cost can be covered by donations 60%-70% of company operation cost can be covered by donations Below 60% of company operation cost can be covered by donations -Ensure that project donation targets are reached
-Try to minimize operational costs

Customer

TRANSPARENCY OF PROJECT DOCUMENTATION Documentation of project progress must be available to the public 95% of project documentations must be posted after 1 month of project completion 85%-94% of project documentations must be posted after 1 month of project completion Below 85% of project documentations should be posted after 1 month of project completion -Create team to continuously update project documentations and post on website
-Provide donors with documentation on how their donations were spent
PROVIDE A SATISFACTORY CUSTOMER EXPERIENCE Review from project volunteers Average an 8./10 or above on the volunteer project evaluation Average anything between 6/10-8/10 on the volunteer project evaluation Average a 6/10 or below on the volunteer project evaluation -Create meaningful and impactful projects to get involved in
-Have the ground team constantly assists and attend to the volunteers needs
PROVIDE A SATISFACTORY COMMUNITY EXPERIENCE Review from community receiving relief Average an 8./10 or above on the community project evaluation Average anything between 6/10-8/10 on the community project evaluation Average a 6/10 or below on the community project evaluation -Create meaningful and impactful projects for communities
-Implement projects well
POST-PROJECT DONOR MANAGEMENT AND RELATION BUILDING Donor appreciation and recognition sent All donors and contributors should receive proper appreciation and recognition notices at most 1 month after project completion 5% or less of donors and contributors have not been sent proper appreciation and recognition notices More than 5% of donors and contributors have not been sent proper appreciation and recognition notices -Create team to immediately inform donors and contributors of our appreciation for their work
-Track donor and contributor notices

Internal Business Process

OBJECTIVE MEASURES GREEN TARGET YELLOW TARGET RED TARGET INITIATIVES
PROJECTS COMPLETED ON TIME Project turnover time 90% of all projects should be completed on time 85%-94% of projects are completed on time Below 85% of project are completed on time -Ensure that ground team and volunteers are always engaged and motivated
-Ensure that training and materials are readily available
-Try to prevent delays in project timeline
PROJECTS COMPLETED ON BUDGET Project costs incurred Stay within allocated budget Exceeding the allocated budget by 5% Exceeding the allocated budget by more than 5% -Plan the costs wisely
-Monitor the costs regularly
AVAILABILITY OF PROJECTS Planning accuracy and speed of project rollout Possible projects should be rolled out for posting within 4 hours of when the disaster strikes Possible projects have not yet been rolled out after 4-10 hours of when the disaster strikes Possible projects have not yet been rolled out after 10 hours of when the disaster strikes -Be vigilant during clamity seasons
-Always be up to date with weather forecasts
-Preempt disasters and plan ahead especially for commonly affected areas
AVAILABILITY OF RESOURCES AND ASSETS NEEDED BY ORGANIZATION On-time delivery of requested resources and assets 85% of resources should be delivered on time 75%-85% of resources are delivered on time Less than 75% of resources are delivered on time -Prioritize resource usage
-Engage with suppliers regularly

Learning and Growth
